# Executive Map Book — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

The **Executive Map Book** is an interactive, internal view of **network maps** and **executive narratives**—use it to prepare for conversations that need geographic context, footprint clarity, or a crisp “why GPC” story grounded in the map experience.

## Key ideas

- Use the map as a **guide** for discussion, not a substitute for **account research** in Salesforce.
- **Executive** and **technical** views may differ; know your audience before you walk through the UI.
- When in doubt, **confirm** facts with the latest operational and product sources—maps can lag reality.

## In the app

Launch **Executive Map Book** from the sidebar for the live experience. This PDF is a printable companion only.
