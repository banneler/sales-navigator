# Connectivity & Access — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module focuses on **connectivity and access** products: when to position **DIA vs shared/SIA**, how latency and SLAs show up in customer language, and how to qualify technical constraints without overpromising.

## Key ideas

- Match **access type** to **application and risk tolerance**—not every site needs DIA.
- **Discovery** on paths, diversity, and performance needs drives better quotes and fewer rework cycles.
- **Cost and SLA** conversations should stay tied to **documented** offers—confirm with quoting/pricing when unsure.
