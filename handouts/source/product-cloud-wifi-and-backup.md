# Cloud, Wi-Fi & Backup — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module ties together **cloud-oriented**, **Wi-Fi**, and **backup/recovery** themes—how customers describe problems, how GPC solutions map to those problems, and what to validate before you commit to architectures or timelines.

## Key ideas

- **Workload and recovery** goals (RPO/RTO) belong in discovery—not as an afterthought on the quote.
- **Wi-Fi** is rarely “just APs”; site survey, density, and interference matter for real outcomes.
- **Partners and cloud services** should follow **approved** SKUs and design patterns—coordinate with specialists.
