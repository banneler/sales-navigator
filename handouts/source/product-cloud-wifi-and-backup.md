## Cloud Connect (positioning)

- **Outcomes:** Predictable performance to major clouds (**AWS, Azure, Google, Oracle, IBM, SAP, Nutanix, Salesforce, Alibaba**, etc.); lower latency than Internet-only paths; security/control; multi-region strategy from one interconnection approach.
- **Mechanism:** **Direct, private** path into GPC’s cloud ecosystem with **Managed Ethernet** as on-ramp; traffic on **secure private** connection vs public Internet only; **up to 10 Gbps** direct connections; ecosystem examples include **Omaha, Chicago, Denver** on product sheet.
- Position **no customer-owned long-haul fabric** when GPC is the transport partner.

**Discovery:** Which clouds/SaaS are critical now and in **12–24 months**? How do they reach them today (DIA, VPN, colo, third-party exchange)? Latency/jitter/capacity pain? Multi-metro or multi-region? Who owns cloud networking and security architecture?

**Caution:** Align scope with **SE** for cross-connect details, diversity, and security expectations—not every site needs full cloud exchange on day one.

## Managed Wi-Fi

- **Outcomes:** Coverage; **guest vs corporate** separation; **managed** lifecycle (design, install, turn-up, maintenance); visibility; optional **branded** guest portal.
- **Proof themes:** **10 Mbps–2 Gbps** Internet-backed designs; engineering-sized density/coverage; integrated guest/private over secure connection; indoor/outdoor APs; **next-gen firewall**, routing, **IDS/IPS**, multiple SSIDs; analytics/reporting; branded guest login.

**Discovery:** Site count; indoor/outdoor; user types and concurrent devices; guest vs private SSID; compliance (HIPAA segmentation, PCI for guest); throughput/app mix; who operates Wi-Fi today; analytics/captive-portal needs.

**Caution:** Site survey and AP counts belong with **engineering**—don’t promise density from a first call.

## Wireless Internet Backup (5G / LTE-class)

- **Outcomes:** Continuity when **fiber or primary access** fails; keep POS, ERP, voice, security, cloud apps; **prioritization**; **NOC** monitoring/alerting; optional **battery**; **unlimited data** positioning per product sheet—**confirm current commercial terms with quoting**.

**Discovery:** What happens on outage today—manual failover, none, or aging 4G? Which apps must survive? RTO in minutes vs hours? Alerting and ownership? Site constraints for **outdoor antenna** or router placement? Battery interest?

**5G honesty:** Performance varies by site; collateral may describe **tiered speeds** after monthly thresholds and **deprioritization** when busy—state it clearly.

---

## Landmines

- **Cloud:** Over-promise cross-connect/diversity/security without SE alignment.
- **Wi-Fi:** Promise AP counts from discovery alone.
- **Backup:** Quote **unlimited** or battery duration without **quoting** confirmation on current SKUs.
- **Training videos:** `.url` shortcuts in extracted training folders may require **on-network** access—follow IT guidance.
