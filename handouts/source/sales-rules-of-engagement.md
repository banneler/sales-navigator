# Sales Rules of Engagement — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module covers **official GPC sales governance**: Salesforce as the system of record, account ownership and activity windows (general vs strategic accounts), inbound lead follow-up expectations, and how to request transfers or escalate edge cases—without reassigning records unilaterally.

## Key ideas

- **Search before you create** accounts or contacts to avoid duplicates and ownership conflicts.
- **Activity windows** help keep idle records workable; strategic and E-Rate entities follow different rules.
- **Inbound leads** have a defined follow-up clock—know the difference vs self-sourced prospecting.
- **Transfers** go through your manager and sales leadership in writing after alignment with the current owner.
