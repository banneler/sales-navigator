# Sales Operations & Approvals — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module is an **internal playbook** for when GPC Solutions hit **approval**, how **change orders** differ from MAC / disconnect / cancel paths, and how **costing routing** behaves at the Solution Site level. Pair it with the Approval Requirements Matrix, PMO/SF Admin/CRC handoffs, and OBR materials referenced in the module.

## Key ideas

- Approvals are **site- and service-aware**—leadership may scrutinize configuration and quote math, not a single headline number.
- **Change orders** apply in a specific window relative to **Closed/Won** and **billing start**; after billing, different flows apply.
- **Costing routing** can force manual validation—know when automation is skipped and why.
