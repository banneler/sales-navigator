# UC, Voice & Collaboration — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module addresses **unified communications, voice, and collaboration**: how to run discovery on **users, sites, and migration risk**, how GPC’s offers fit common buyer journeys, and how to stay aligned with **pricing and sizing** teams on quotes and renewals.

## Key ideas

- **Porting, E911, and regulatory** details can break deals—surface them early.
- **Internal sizing** and rate discussions belong in **approved** channels—never email ad-hoc rates from memory.
- **Collaboration** tools change often—pair battle cards with **current** product marketing.
