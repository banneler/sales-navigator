# UC, Voice & Collaboration — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

**Internal pricing books** for UC, Contact Center, and voice—**never email rates from memory**. **Concurrent** licensing, add-on channels, archiving tiers, **bursting**, and PS lines require **AE / pricing desk** alignment.

## Unified Communications (UC)

- One platform: PBX, AI-assisted calling, messaging, SMS/MMS to customers, HD meetings with AI recap, optional Contact Center, file sync; mobile/desktop apps.
- **Plans:** Essential/Pro smaller file allowances; **Unite Enterprise** up to **200 GB** per user in brochure; lower tiers **50 GB / 5 GB**—mix/match and flexible contracts.
- **Triple Guard Security™:** secure datacenters, certified security team, encryption, password management, 2FA.
- **Capabilities (examples):** Cloud PBX 100+ features, spam protection, single admin; **AI Call Recap**; file backup/recovery; team messaging + external SMS/MMS; **AI Meeting Recap**; **Advanced Hunt Groups**; optional full **Contact Center**.
- **Archiving:** free **30-day** baseline—longer retention is **tiered**; **confirm tier with account team**—do not quote years of retention on first call without pricing.

**Discovery:** How do employees communicate today? Where do calls, texts, voicemails live? Post-meeting notes automated? Compliance/retention for calls/chats? File backup and ransomware recovery posture?

## UC with Microsoft Teams

- Keep **Teams** as hub; GPC **enterprise-grade cloud PBX** from Teams dialer.
- **Microsoft Teams licenses** and **Phone System** are purchased **separately**—set expectations on the **joint licensing** picture.
- **Handoff:** UC/Teams architects for coexistence, porting, Phone System prerequisites; coordinate CSP context if Teams is bought elsewhere.

**Discovery:** Teams standard for chat/meetings? Teams Phone today or bridged PBX? Hunt groups, queues, paging, hot desking, CRM screen pop? Presence sync between queues and Teams status?

## Contact Center (CC)

- Same UC platform; one app for employee + customer interactions.
- **CC Pro** / **CC Elite** **concurrent** seats—bundled with UC or standalone; **Elite** extends capability—**confirm current matrix**.
- Digital channels, Dynamic Notifications often **add-ons**; **bursting/elastic** = **internal** sizing with AE/pricing.
- **Handoff:** Specialists for custom reports, bespoke IVR, custom integrations (prof services in datasheet); bursting with AE/pricing.

**Discovery:** Volume and channel mix? Sites and WFH agents? SLAs and abandon rates? CRM and WFM? Recording/compliance? Outbound vs inbound?

## Voice (non-UC)

- **SIP:** VoIP between customer PBX and GPC toward PSTN; consolidate voice and data; **managed eSBC** on-site.
- **PRI:** digital trunking; multiple paths/features (Caller ID, etc.).
- **POTS:** landline; **911** where applicable independent of mobile battery.

**Discovery:** PBX or key system? SIP vs TDM? Path counts and growth? Fax/specialty lines? Elevator/alarm lines that must stay POTS?

**Handoff:** Voice engineering for eSBC placement, SIP interoperability, cutover windows.

## Competitive themes

- Whole-business workflow vs meeting-only SKUs; local support; **99.999%** uptime—translate to **minutes/year** in customer language.

## Landmines

- Imply one GPC SKU replaces **all** Microsoft licensing.
- Email **internal rate tables** to customers.
- Quote CC, bursting, or archiving years from **memory** or napkin math.
- Archiving: quote retention years on first call without account team.
