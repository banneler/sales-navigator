# Security & SD-WAN — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module covers **managed security** and **SD-WAN** positioning: pain themes, how GPC wraps services, qualifying questions, and when to pull **SE/PM** or **quoting** into the thread. Treat internal fee schedules and SKUs as **non–customer-facing** until validated and approved.

## Key ideas

- **Pain → wrap → questions → proof** is a safer arc than leading with vendor logos.
- **Verify** claims, awards, and stats before **external** use—especially in regulated or RFP-heavy deals.
- **Sizing and design** should stay aligned with quoting teams; avoid ad-hoc rates from memory.
