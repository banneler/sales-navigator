## Managed Firewall & MNS (umbrella)

- **Managed Firewall:** Fortinet® FortiGate with Enterprise/Unified Threat Protection, **24/7 SOC**, IPS, malware prevention, **ZTNA** for hybrid access, centralized cloud management, compliance-oriented logging/reporting, migration from existing configs.
- **MNS** umbrella includes: (1) Managed Firewall; (2) **Firewall Management** of **customer-owned** gear (Cisco, Fortinet, Palo Alto, etc.); (3) Firewall + **Managed SIEM**; (4) **Assessments** and vulnerability scanning; (5) **Managed Email Security** (M365/Slack-oriented).
- Position **MNS** when buyers need SIEM, multi-vendor firewall ops, email security, or assessments—not only a new appliance.

**Buying triggers:** audit/compliance pressure; no 24/7 security bench; recent incident; vendor sprawl; hybrid workforce needing ZTNA; firewall refresh.

## DDoS protection

- Cloud scrubbing; traffic analysis; bandwidth thresholds; expert mitigation.
- **Proactive:** **24×7 automated** monitoring and mitigation.
- **On-demand:** monitoring/notifications; mitigation **weekdays 8–17** on the on-demand SKU—**qualify explicitly**; not 24×7 mitigation on that SKU.
- Scale **10 Mbps–100 Gbps**; BGP FlowSpec/TMS on proactive paths; portal reporting.

**Discovery:** What must stay reachable under attack? Past saturation or outage? Always-on vs cost-sensitive on-demand?

## SD-WAN (VMware VeloCloud™)

- Fits **at least one site on GPC fiber** and **multi-site** (including off-net U.S. branches).
- Replaces/augments MPLS, reduces backhaul, improves cloud/SaaS breakout, consolidates routing/VPN/optional security at edge—**no up-front CPE purchase** in managed model.
- **Next-Gen Firewall / IDS** lines may be **optional** add-ons—**confirm scope in every quote.**

**Discovery:** Total locations and on-GPC vs off-net? MPLS vs Internet VPN today? Local breakout vs full backhaul? Bandwidth per site? **12–24** month timeline? Mission-critical SaaS? Apps in DC vs cloud?

**Positioning:** “Local GPC support plus a leading WAN-edge platform vs national carriers—**positioning**, not unverified specs.”

## Handoff to engineering / security

Bring: site list, WAN diagram, bandwidths, firewall inventory, compliance drivers, success criteria—especially for mixed-vendor firewall management; high-scale DC; complex SD-WAN routing/HA; DDoS with BGP/ASN or custom thresholds; regulated environments.

## Do not promise

- Fixed firewall/IPS/SSL throughput as guarantees (“up to” varies by features).
- That GPC “certifies” compliance alone.
- **DDoS:** blur proactive vs on-demand mitigation windows.
- **SD-WAN:** every security feature in base SKU; unlimited app-ID accuracy; MPLS disappears overnight without migration; feature parity with competitor SD-WAN without **SE** review.
- **General:** “100% uptime,” “zero breaches,” unqualified “leader” claims—defer SLA, design, and dollars to specialists.
- **Internal pricing** to customers without approval.
