# Competitive Positioning — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module teaches a **disciplined competitive conversation**: discovery before claims, mapping **competitor → GPC response**, avoiding landmines, using **proof** responsibly, and knowing when to engage **SE/PM**. Sections align to metro, fiber vs satellite, M&A, and technology battle-card playbooks.

## Key ideas

- **Discovery before claims**—map strengths only after you understand their reality.
- **No personal attacks**; avoid citing battle-card stats **externally** without confirming currency.
- **Fiber vs LEO** and **M&A** stories must stay factual and customer-centric—tie to outcomes, not slogans.
