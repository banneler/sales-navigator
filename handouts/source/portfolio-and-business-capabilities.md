# Portfolio & Business Capabilities — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module frames **what GPC offers** at a portfolio level: how to describe **capabilities** (not just products), how pieces fit together in customer conversations, and how to avoid “speeds and feeds” when the buyer cares about outcomes and risk.

## Key ideas

- Lead with **business outcomes** and **proof**; attach products and services as the how.
- **Portfolio language** should stay consistent with marketing and product—avoid inventing positioning on the fly.
- Use this module to **align** with specialists (SE, PM, quoting) when the deal crosses domains.
