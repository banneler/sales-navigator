# Getting Started — Handout

*Sales-Navigator · Great Plains Communications*

## At a glance

This module orients you to **Sales-Navigator**: how the sidebar is organized, what a typical module looks like (summary, deep-dive sections, scenarios, knowledge checks), and how to use the quick tour.

## What to remember

- Training is **modular**—jump to what you need and revisit anytime.
- **Scenarios** and **knowledge checks** are practice, not graded tests; use them to rehearse real conversations.
- The **Executive Map Book** lives under its own nav item for maps and executive storylines.

## In the app

Complete the **Getting started** tour in the app for the interactive walkthrough. This PDF is a printable companion only.
