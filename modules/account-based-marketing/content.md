## Why ABM here

This module is intentionally simple: it anchors on the ABM campaign visual and keeps teams aligned on one shared 90-day sequence.

## 90-day sequence

ABM campaign: 90-day C-level sequence (reference Gantt)

## How to use this module

1. Use the chart as the source of truth for sequence order and month-by-month coverage.
2. Coach to the lane labels first (Sales Prospecting vs Marketing Air Cover), then to individual steps.
3. If the official ABM SOP changes, update this page to match the visual and sequence labels.

## Sequence lanes (sales & marketing)

### Sales prospecting

The chart shows an **18-touch** sequence across Month 1, Month 2, and Month 3, including stages such as intro, calls, case study, follow-up, meeting ask, OBR, alignment, and final call.

### Marketing air cover

Month 1 is labeled **LinkedIn & Google Ads (Awareness)**, Month 2 is **Direct Mail (Validation)**, and Month 3 is **Retargeting (Conversion)**, with an optional **Brochure Insert Copy** callout between Month 1 and Month 2.
