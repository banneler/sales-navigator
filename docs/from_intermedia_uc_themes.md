## `UCaaS_BYOC`

- **Unified “Ascend” / UC stack** — one platform for business voice (100+ features), SMS/company messaging, chat, HD meetings, web fax, and file sync/share with desktop, mobile, and browser entry points.
- **AI as a core story** — AI Call Recap, AI Meeting Recap, AI Assistant, supervisor/agent-assist style capabilities, and roadmap items (e.g. AI Receptionist, AI Topics/Trends “coming soon”), framed as productivity and insight.
- **DataHub + integrations** — analytics/reporting, “Extend”/API positioning, CRM screen pops, and tying communications data to AI and business apps.
- **Security, uptime, compliance** — 99.999% SLA narrative, Triple Guard Security™, and archiving with retention from 30 days up to 10 years.
- **Contact Center add-on** — omnichannel CX (voice, SMS, chat, email, WhatsApp), AI for routing/sentiment/summaries, and deep CRM/ERP integrations (Salesforce, Zendesk, Dynamics, HubSpot, NetSuite, etc.).
- **Microsoft Teams embedded** — voice/SMS and CC inside Teams; no Teams Phone license positioning; built-in redundancy if Teams is unavailable; archiving across UC + Teams data.
- **Commercial packaging** — Mix & Match tiers (Express / Essentials / Pro / Enterprise) with per-user tailoring; separate **Ascend for Teams** mix & match matrices.
- **device SKUs/matrices** — desk phones, DECT/conference, headsets, ATAs, and webcam/speakerphone comparison-style content.
- **Cloud modernization pitch** — legacy PBX pain points vs. cloud; bundled pricing metaphor; AI as continuously improving capability; mobile-first workforce.
- **End-customer narrative** — “one intelligent platform,” better employee + customer experiences, flexibility (mix & match, contracts), and SMS on business numbers.
- **End-user / rollout training** — Ascend app navigation (chat, meetings, files, calling, SMS); install/sign-in; Teams app install, pin, desktop plugin for calling; SMS and calling modes; Teams mobile limitations called out.
- **Teams admin/user training** — step-by-step for embedded app + plugin; company vs personal SMS; sync between Ascend mobile and Teams mobile for calling.

**Gaps / noise:** Extraction is OCR-heavy (typos, broken words, repeated labels like “CRM SCREEN POPS”), brochure content overlaps across PDFs, tier matrices dominate several sections (light on narrative), and naming shifts (**UC**, **Ascend**, occasional **Unite** in footnotes) make unified messaging harder to read from text alone.

## `UCaaS_IPN`

- **Unified “Ascend / UC” story** — One intelligent platform for voice, chat, meetings, SMS, file sharing, and AI; emphasis on employee productivity, customer experience, and operational outcomes (plus **99.999% SLA** and **Triple Guard Security** in broader UC messaging).
- **Full-stack UC capabilities** — **100+** business voice features, **Meet**, SMS/company messaging (NA caveats in places), web fax, file sharing with per-tier storage, **AI Call Recap**, **AI Meeting Recap**, **AI Assistant**, and roadmap items such as **AI Agent Receptionist**.
- **Microsoft Teams (embedded)** — **UC for Microsoft Teams**: enterprise voice and SMS inside Teams, **no Teams Phone license** required in positioning; **desktop plugin** ties telephony to Teams; **Teams mobile** has documented limits (e.g., some features only via Ascend mobile).
- **Contact Center** — Omnichannel CX (voice, SMS, chat, email, WhatsApp), AI-assisted routing and insights, optional **softphone** positioning; can pair with UC for Teams for a single-app story.
- **Archiving** — **30-day** rolling retention called out as included/baseline in several places, with **1–10 year** paid tiers; capture of calls, chats, meetings, SMS, etc.; compliance and legal use cases (e.g., HIPAA/FINRA-style framing in the archiving flyer).
- **CRM & business integrations** — **Ascend + Contact Center** integrations with **Salesforce, Zendesk, Dynamics, HubSpot, NetSuite**, etc.; **Teams** integration block listing calling, SMS, hunt groups, archiving backup, omnichannel; **DataHub** / **Extend** analytics and APIs in the main UC brochure section.
- **“Mix & Match” packaging** — Tiered **Express / Essentials / Pro / Enterprise** matrices for standard UC and **Ascend for Teams** plans (features differ by row: AI Call Recap, hunt groups, archiving rows, video limits, etc.).
- **Endpoints & hardware** — **Phones & devices** comparison (Yealink, Fanvil, conference phones, DECT, ATAs, headsets, cameras) supporting the “plug-and-play” phone-system narrative.
- **End-user & admin-facing guides** — **Ascend** desktop/mobile quick start (chat, meetings, files, calling modes) and **Ascend for Teams Embedded** install (pin app, install plugin, SMS flows, mobile sync).
- **Upgrade / modernization** — “Upgrade to Ascend” storyline: cost/efficiency, richer PBX and video, CC options, AI, company SMS, Teams integration, white-glove migration language.
- **Sales narrative (pitch deck)** — Legacy PBX pain vs cloud; **TCO-style** comparison; **AI** as differentiator; **free 30-day archiving** with upsell to longer retention; **Teams** as collaboration layer with Ascend for telephony/CX/archiving.

## `UC for Teams Embedded Solution - Voice, SMS, Contact Center, and AI - All Inside Teams`

- **Embedded Teams:** Positions UC, Contact Center, archiving, and AI inside Microsoft Teams—enterprise phone, SMS, AI Call Recap, and optional CC without a Microsoft Teams Phone license; collaboration stays in Teams while calling and SMS run through the **UC tab** on desktop, with redundancy via UC web/mobile if Teams has connectivity issues.
- **Embedded Teams (mobile):** FAQs state business calls work from the Teams mobile app with the UC app installed; **SMS in the Teams mobile app is not available yet** (voice-only mobile; SMS planned).
- **CC with Teams:** Materials describe **UC for Teams Advanced** plus CC—omnichannel (voice, SMS, chat, email, etc.), **Teams Desktop plugin** for Contact Center, AI sentiment/summaries/routing/analytics; can be deployed **with UC** or **added later** as you grow.
- **MSP/VAR pitch:** Stresses **market scale** (cloud telephony growth, many users still not on cloud voice), **contract renewals** as a migration moment, **~6% Teams Phone adoption** vs most Teams users lacking integrated business calling/SMS, and partner benefits: **single provider** for Voice, CC, Archiving, and Teams; included advanced calling + SMS; built-in archiving; simpler administration; **continuity when Teams is down**.
- **Customer deck (PPTX):** Story-driven pitch—differentiation, Teams Phone gaps, usage stats, **archiving**, **UC tab** flow, **seamless connectivity**, **CC + AI CX**, **unified archiving**, compliance/use cases, feature overview + SSO, and **four reasons** (single app, reliability, archiving, local support) plus **one provider** for M365/Teams + UC for Teams Advanced.
- **FAQs (PDF):** What Embedded is; **Embedded vs Direct Routing** (no Teams Phone license on Embedded; DR uses Microsoft infra/native dialer); integration via UC tab; presence sync; benefits; how to start (sales demo/setup).

**PPTX vs PDF:** The **customer deck `.pptx`** is the **slide-by-slide pitch** (positioning, stats, UX, CC, archiving); the **PDFs** are **reference sheets** (UC+CC matrices, CC Advanced), **FAQ answers**, and **MSP/VAR pitch guide**—better for exact wording, Q&A, and partner enablement than for live deck delivery.

**Best use for GPC internal training:** Use **MSP/VAR PDF** and **FAQ PDF** for objections, licensing differences, and partner narrative; use **deck extracts** for customer storyline and demo flow; use **feature matrices** for feature boundaries and Teams vs UC vs CC roles.
